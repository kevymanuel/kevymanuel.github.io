At Ando, a mission-driven fintech startup, acquisition was strong ‚Äî but activation was lagging. While ~90% of new users completed onboarding, fewer than 16% were reaching full activation, which required account funding and at least three transactions within 10 business days. To address this, I led the design and launch of the Impact Center ‚Äî a personalized in-app dashboard that guided users through activation steps, highlighted rewards, and showcased the environmental impact of their banking activity.

üîç Problem Statement

Before the Impact Center:

  • Users were guided through a "To Do's" module that listed incomplete actions but lacked clear value propositions
  • Key activation actions ‚Äî account funding, direct deposit, savings account creation, transactions ‚Äî were not prioritized or tied to reward visibility
  • Rewards (cash back, direct deposit bonuses, referrals) were available, but users had no single place to understand their eligibility or progress
  • The effort to switch primary banking to Ando was high; without visible value and mission alignment, users stalled in activation

Benchmarks & Gaps:

  • Ando's activation rate: 16%, far below the 36% industry benchmark
  • Needed to increase activation without creating friction, while reinforcing Ando's environmental mission

üí° Solution ‚Äì The Impact Center

A centralized, in-app engagement hub to:

  • Clearly show users their remaining activation steps
  • Display potential rewards and progress toward earning them
  • Include a calculator for:
    • Potential cash back earnings
    • Referral and direct deposit rewards
    • CO‚ÇÇ emissions reduced through Ando's environmental projects
  • Reinforce Ando's mission-driven brand by connecting banking activity to tangible environmental benefits

üí¨ Reflection

The Impact Center demonstrated that clear guidance + visible value = higher activation. By combining financial incentives with mission alignment, we addressed both rational and emotional motivators for users to complete their onboarding journey.

This project reinforced my belief that activation levers must connect immediate user value with long-term brand loyalty.

  • Clear guidance + visible value = higher activation - The combination of step-by-step guidance with tangible rewards proved highly effective
  • Mission alignment drives engagement - Connecting banking activity to environmental impact created emotional connection
  • Reusable framework for future features - Created a template for tying rewards and mission-driven content into core banking UX
